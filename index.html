Our Doctors

Michael B. Limberg, M.D.

Dr. Limberg is the region's First Board Certified and Fellowship trained Laser Vision Correction specialist.

While working in New Orleans, he was on the first team to perform Laser Vision Correction in the world.

Since establishing his practice in San Luis Obispo, he has personally performed over 20,000 vision correction procedures and specializes in surgeries designed to free patients from glasses and contact lenses.

Read Full Bio

Dr. Limberg is Fellowship trained in numerous surgical techniques and procedures designed to reconstruct eyes which have had unsuccessful Lasik, cataract or corneal surgeries performed elsewhere.

He has published extensively in leading medical journals and taught many surgical courses in the field of advanced surgical techniques for university and corporate training courses.

He has performed LASIK and refractive lens surgery on physicians from five continents and 19 countries.
